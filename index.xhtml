BOE Access Test

We are in receipt of a CARF requesting your access to BOE. To ensure you are a successful user and comfortable with producing reports, the following test is designed to help you navigate BOE. Please take the test and submit your answers. Your results will be processed by enterprise reporting staff. Should you have any questions, please send an email to BOEXI@tceq.texas.gov.

Instructions

Enterprise Reporting (ER) has nine (9) BOE training module videos available on the Sharenet. The videos are located under TCEQ BOE Users. Total viewing time for the nine (9) videos is less than one (1) hour.

The logon information will be emailed to you within two (2) business days of receipt of your completed quiz.
