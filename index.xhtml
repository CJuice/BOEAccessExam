BOE Access Exam

Instructions

We are in receipt of a CARF requesting your access to BOE. To ensure you are a successful user and comfortable with producing reports, the following exam is designed to help you navigate BOE. Please take the exam and submit your answers. Should you have any questions, please send an email to BOEXI@tceq.texas.gov.

Enterprise Reporting (ER) has nine (9) BOE training module videos available on the Sharenet. The videos are located under TCEQ BOE Users. Total viewing time for the nine (9) videos is less than one (1) hour.

Upon an exam score of 80 percent (%) or greater, ER staff will create your BOE logon. The logon information will be emailed to you within two (2) business days of receipt of your completed exam. Please note, some of the below questions may have more than one choice as the correct answer. Please indicate all correct choices when answering a question.

BOE general information can be located on the Sharenet. Access to Production BOE TCEQ Reports.
